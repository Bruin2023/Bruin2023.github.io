Splinter and Bloom

Hello! My name is Kelly Kingman, and as part of my HTML/CSS project for IS 270, I decided to create a page about my creative work. Splinter and Bloom is an offshoot of an exciting journey that I am on, exploring and developing techniques in mixed media and abstract art. My focus up to this point has been on the creation of collages using everything from paint to paper.

As I explored, I found myself drawn to vibrantly colored abstract art. Inspired by all the delicious colors and electric designs that I found by other artists on Instagram and the Internet, I decided to sign up for a course that will teach me how to create popping abstract art tucked into the pages of a my own sketchbook.

I am very excited to take Suzanne's 31 Bright and Fun Sketchbook Paintings course!

How I used HTML & CSS to Create My Page

I had a lot of fun searching ChatGPT for different ways to alter and dress up my HTML page. I didn't get it exactly the way I wanted because of time, but I did change and add a few things. Here are just a few of the things I did:

head: I split the page into two with a top header and a main content section.

section: I added a second image with a hyperlink to another website and I set the "background-color".

img: I added borders around images.

body: I set the "font-family" and "background-color".

a and a:hover: I changed the color, hover color, and added an underline to my hyperlink.
